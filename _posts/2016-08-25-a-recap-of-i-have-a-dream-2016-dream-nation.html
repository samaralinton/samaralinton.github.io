“To make any kind of progress, we need to be able to imagine a different reality, and then we need to believe that that reality is possible” – Tali Sharot

Every year, Dream Nation hosts I Have A Dream, a conference which celebrates Practical Dreamers and equips us with the insight needed to achieve our own goals. Hosted by the charming Nissy Tee, the evening covered important topics including leadership, financial literacy, productivity, emotional intelligence and personal branding. We were treated with a live spoken word performance by Jolade and the mesmerising vocals of Barbella and, in true Dream Nation style, there was opportunity to network and build relationships.
